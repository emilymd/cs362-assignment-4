BATTLESHIP

Instructions:
  1. Set your ships! In the "Place Ships" box, place one ship of each type, and decide whether or not you want it to be vertical.
  2. Attack your opponent's ships! Click on the opponent's grid to place your attack. See the legend to find out how you did.
  3. CAPTAIN'S QUARTERS: All ships have a square called the Captain's Quarters. Hitting this square once on Minesweeper will instantly sink it. Hitting it twice on the other ships will instantly sink all the remaining squares of this ship!
  4. SONAR PULSE: This allows you to reveal hidden ships! After you sink one ship, you're able to use this. Check the box and click a square to see if a ship is revealed (black square) or not (gray square). Careful, you may only use this twice a game!
  5. Repeat until you win or lose! Click the NEW GAME button to start over and have fun!
